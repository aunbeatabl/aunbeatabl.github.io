Directed Reading Programs


During Spring 2025 I participated in the Berkeley Math Directed Reading Program, reading about the Theory of Combinatorial Species under the mentorship of graduate student Saud Molaib. This program culminated in a final project for which I wrote an expository paper and made a presentation about the Theory of Combinatorial Species and a proof of the Lagrange Inversion Formula.
